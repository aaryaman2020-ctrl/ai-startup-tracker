## Summary: Key Themes & Strategic Takeaways

### The 5 Laws of AI Startup Success (2025–2026)

1. **Vertical depth beats horizontal breadth.** Generic tools die; vertically embedded tools survive.
2. **Workflow ownership is the moat.** Products embedded in daily operations achieve NRR >130%.
3. **Data compounding creates defensibility.** Each customer interaction should make your model smarter.
4. **Agentic AI is the frontier.** Copilots will commoditize. Autonomous agents are defensible.
5. **Serve underserved buyers.** Foundation model companies won't build for SMBs in Nigeria or mid-market insurance companies in the Midwest.
